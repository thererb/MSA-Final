About Us

Our Purpose and more!

October 30, 2023 by Robert

After 5 years since the first title and 3 years since the Miles standalone, Marvel's Spider-Man 2 shows that Insomniac isn't slowing down.

Our "About Us" page is a glimpse into the heart and soul of our website, dedicated to fostering a community of passionate voices. Our purpose is clear - we've crafted this platform as a space where individuals, particularly from the vibrant city of Chicago, can share their unique perspectives on games, films, anime, television, sports, comics, and technology. Through insightful review-based articles, our contributors delve into their experiences, offering a diverse range of opinions that resonate with our readers. It's not just a platform; it's a collective of stories, experiences, and insights. From the bustling streets of Chicago to diverse backgrounds and unique life journeys, we celebrate the richness that each contributor brings to our digital canvas. Our hope is that, through this exploration of who we are, readers and creators will find a sense of belonging, inspiration, and a shared passion for the ever-evolving world of entertainment and technology. Join us in shaping the future of this community, where every voice is heard, appreciated, and celebrated.

Audience

At the core of our vision is our audience - a dynamic group of middle-aged to young adults, and even younger readers, seeking engaging content created by peers within the same age spectrum. To ensure inclusivity, we're exploring ideas like categorizing content to distinguish mature themes from kid-friendly material. Our mission is to connect with individuals who share common ground, whether it's their connection to Chicago, experiences of more challenging upbringings, or any aspect that lends a unique perspective. This "About Us" page is an invitation to join our vibrant community, where creators' stories take center stage, influencing and enhancing discussions on the topics we're passionate about.
